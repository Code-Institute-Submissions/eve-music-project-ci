The all-female collective comprised of Lauren, Jessica and Micu was formed four years ago when three friends decided to take their passion for electronic music one step further. Holding a monthly residency in Pygmalion and frequenting the top Irish festivals and venues as well as travelling to play in the legendary Katerblau, Berlin, the girls have made a name for themselves on the electronic music scene - making waves with their infectious energy and presence throughout their DJ sets.

By hosting parties with an all female line-up Eve allows the girls playing to have full creative control over their night from start to finish, enabling them to bring their audience on a musical journey. The main focus of Eve will be music, but we will be exploring other creative avenues along the way from yoga to photography and everything in between - sharing interesting things that females are doing in Ireland and all over the globe. One day we hope to combine all of these different creative elements at our parties creating an exhibition of female talent.
